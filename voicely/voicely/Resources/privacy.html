3. AI-Generated Voice Content

Content Processing: When you input text for voice generation, this information is processed by our AI service providers, including the MiniMax-02-HD model, to create personalized audio. The generated audio is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Data Retention: Your input text is processed in real-time and is not stored on our servers. Generated audio remains on your device and is subject to your device's storage management.
